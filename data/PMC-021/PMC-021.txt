Piezoelectric Property : Yes

Evidence             :
• First quantitative experimental demonstration of the direct piezoelectric effect in L-leucine single crystals.
• Extensive electromechanical characterization confirms the intrinsic piezoelectricity of L-leucine.
• The crystal belongs to the non-centrosymmetric space group P21 (No. 4), which permits piezoelectricity.
• The study identifies L-leucine as a promising piezoactive bioelectret for electromechanical and energy-harvesting applications.
