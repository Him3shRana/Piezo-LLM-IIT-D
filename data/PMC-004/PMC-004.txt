Molecule Name: 2-Methyl-1H-benzimidazole (MBI)

Chemical Formula: C₈H₈N₂

Crystal Type: Organic hydrogen-bonded molecular ferroelectric crystal (γ-polymorph)

Piezoelectric Property: Yes

Evidence: The crystal adopts the non-centrosymmetric Pn (No. 7) space group (polar point group m) and exhibits experimentally verified ferroelectricity due to proton-transfer along hydrogen-bonded chains. Ferroelectricity and polarization switching are demonstrated in the paper, making piezoelectricity symmetry-allowed.

Research Paper: Above-room-temperature ferroelectricity and antiferroelectricity in benzimidazoles, Nature Communications, 2012, 3, 1308. DOI: 10.1038/ncomms2322

Piezoelectric Coefficient: Not reported (NR)
