Crystal ID: PMC-002

Molecule Name: β-Glycine (Beta Glycine)

Chemical Formula: C2H5NO2

Crystal Type: Organic Molecular Crystal (Single Crystal)

Piezoelectric Property: Yes

Evidence:
The research experimentally confirms the piezoelectric behavior of β-glycine. β-Glycine/PCL composite films exhibited an effective piezoelectric coefficient of 10.3 pm/V and were successfully used as the active material in a piezoelectric nanogenerator (PENG). The device generated electrical output under mechanical pressure and ultrasound stimulation, demonstrating the intrinsic piezoelectric nature of β-glycine crystals.
