Piezoelectric Property : Yes

Evidence             :
• Quantitative experimental measurements of the piezoelectric constants were reported for Rochelle salt single crystals.
• Rochelle salt is one of the earliest known molecular crystals exhibiting both piezoelectricity and ferroelectricity.
• Rochelle salt crystallizes in the ferroelectric monoclinic phase with the non-centrosymmetric space group P21 (No. 4), which is crystallographically compatible with piezoelectricity.
• The crystal has been extensively studied as a benchmark molecular piezoelectric material.
