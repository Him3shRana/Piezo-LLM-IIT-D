Evidence:
The paper experimentally confirms that Imidazolium D-camphorsulfonate is a ferroelectric molecular crystal exhibiting piezoelectricity. Evidence includes ferroelectric polarization (P-E hysteresis loops), piezoresponse force microscopy (PFM) domain imaging and switching, and direct piezoelectric coefficient (d33) measurements.
