Remarks
-------
The CIF corresponds to the pure N-Acetyl-L-Tryptophan crystal
(historical single-crystal structure, refcode ACLTRY, 1977).
In the piezoelectric study (JACS 2023), L-AcW was reported to adopt
the same packing as this previously reported structure (identical
PXRD patterns) and was identified as the highest-performing acetylated
amino acid crystal, whose nanogenerator directly powered an LED.
This crystal structure is the appropriate reference CIF for the
pure material.
