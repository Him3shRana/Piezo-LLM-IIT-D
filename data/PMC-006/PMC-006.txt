Molecule Name: 5,6-Dichloro-2-methyl-1H-benzimidazole (DC-MBI)

Chemical Formula: C₈H₆Cl₂N₂

Crystal Type: Organic hydrogen-bonded molecular ferroelectric crystal (chlorinated benzimidazole derivative)

Piezoelectric Property: Yes

Evidence: DC-MBI crystallizes in the non-centrosymmetric polar space group Pca2₁ (No. 29) and exhibits experimentally demonstrated ferroelectricity with switchable spontaneous polarization. Since all ferroelectric crystals are inherently piezoelectric, DC-MBI is classified as a piezoelectric molecular crystal. The paper experimentally verifies its ferroelectric behavior through polarization measurements and domain studies.

Research Paper: Above-room-temperature ferroelectricity and antiferroelectricity in benzimidazoles, Nature Communications, 2012, 3, 1308. DOI: 10.1038/ncomms2322
